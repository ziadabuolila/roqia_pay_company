1- What is Vision Pay Payment Solutions and Software Company?

It is a company specializing in providing advanced electronic payment solutions and software services that support the digital transformation of institutions and individuals with the highest standards of quality and security.

2- What services does your company provide in the field of financial and technical solutions?

We offer electronic payment gateways, digital wallets, electronic point of sale (POS), financial collection solutions, and customized software for various sectors such as commerce, education, and government.

3- How do I benefit from Vision's e-payment services?

By contacting our customer service team or via the official website, we can analyze your technical needs and choose the appropriate solution for you.

4- What is the payment method through the portals of Vision Pay for Payment Solutions and Software Company?

After integrating a payment gateway with your website or app, your customers can choose a product or service and then pay securely online using credit cards, digital wallets, or bank transfers.

5- Does Roaya Pay provide electronic points of sale (POS) for stores?

Yes, we provide modern point-of-sale devices to easily accept payments via bank cards or e-wallets.

6- What payment methods does Roaya Pay support?

We support all popular payment methods: Visa, MasterCard, Meeza, e-wallets such as Vodafone Cash and Orange Cash, as well as electronic installment services.

7- Are Roaya's Pay services available to individuals or only to companies?

Our services are available to individuals, startups, e-commerce stores, and government and private institutions.

8- Can I pay by mobile phone using Roaya Pay services?

Sure! Roaya Pay offers smart mobile payment solutions through its easy-to-use and secure app.

9- How secure are transactions with Roaya Pay Payment Integrated Solutions and Software?

We adhere to the highest global security standards (PCI DSS), encrypting and protecting data against any breach to ensure a reliable payment experience.

10- Does Roaya Pay provide ongoing technical support to customers?

Yes, our team is available 24/7 to ensure continuity of operations and support customers with all their inquiries and technical issues.
